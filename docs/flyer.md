# ALVer — Flyer Creative Brief

Three angles for a promotional flyer. Each can stand alone or be combined.

---

## Angle 1: "Your vote, your data"

**Headline:** Your vote. Your data. Your assembly.

**Subline:** ALVer brings general assembly meetings into the digital age — without handing your data to anyone.

**Key points:**
- Vote live from your phone, no app needed
- Your cooperative's data stays in its own sovereign space — not on our servers
- Verified identity via eID, paperless by design

**Visual concept:** Clean minimal layout. A phone showing a live poll result alongside a bold typographic treatment of "ADOPTED". Subtle W3DS / eCommons branding in the footer. Cooperative-warm colour palette (terracotta, cream, green).

**Best for:** Tech-aware audiences, privacy-conscious cooperatives, W3DS ecosystem events.

---

## Angle 2: "Show up or send your vote"

**Headline:** Can't make it? Your voice still counts.

**Subline:** Pre-register, give a mandate, vote live — ALVer handles it all so your general assembly runs smoothly.

**Key points:**
- Pre-register attendance or give a mandate in 30 seconds
- Check in at the door with a QR code scan
- Live polls with instant results on screen

**Visual concept:** Split screen — left side shows someone at home on their phone giving a mandate; right side shows the meeting room with a QR code on the projector. Warm, human photography style or friendly illustration. Large headline typography.

**Best for:** General member communication, onboarding flyers handed out at meetings, printed posters at the venue.

---

## Angle 3: "Democracy in your pocket"

**Headline:** General assembly meetings made easy.

**Subline:** ALVer turns any room into a fully digital, transparent voting session — in minutes.

**Key points:**
- No app download — works in any phone browser
- Real-time vote counts visible to everyone
- Full archive of decisions after every meeting

**Visual concept:** Bold, energetic layout. Large number "1" overlaid with "scan" → "2" with "vote" → "3" with "decided". QR code as a visual element. Works well as a square social media card or A5 handout.

**Best for:** Social media, event announcements, outreach to new cooperatives considering the tool.

---

## Suggested copy for all formats

**Tagline (short):** General assembly meetings made easy.

**One-liner:** ALVer is a free, open, privacy-first platform for cooperative general assemblies — pre-registration, live voting, and a permanent record of every decision.

**Call to action options:**
- "Ask your facilitator for the meeting link"
- "Scan to join the meeting"
- "Learn more at [your-cooperative-url]"
