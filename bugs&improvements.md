Later, ideas, questions:

Controls for screen should be grouped together. In a dropdown next/part of Screen button

1. Quorum / consent
Every cooperative bylaw has a quorum requirement — e.g. "voting is only valid if at least 1/3 of members are present." Right now the facilitator has no way to see whether quorum is reached before starting. The app knows all the numbers — checked-in non-aspirants, total members — but never surfaces this. A facilitator could accidentally start a vote that's legally invalid.

- also maybe a way to retry - the same poll but voted again?

- anyways the voting rules should be chosen in the settings

2. Secret ballot
Some decisions legally require anonymous voting — board elections, sensitive motions. Right now every vote is stored with voter_name. For a cooperative that needs to meet Dutch cooperative law, this is a real compliance gap.

3. Minutes export
After every meeting, the secretary writes minutes. The archive has all the data (decisions, tallies, attendees, mandates) but there's no way to get it out as a document. Someone copies it manually right now.

4. Member CSV import
Adding members one by one is fine for 10 people. For a housing cooperative with 80 members it's a blocker. Every new community you onboard hits this wall immediately.
